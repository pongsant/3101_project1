What is Manchester United?

Manchester United is an English football club based in Manchester. The team plays at Old Trafford and has a long history of attacking football, famous players, and loyal fans around the world.

Short timeline

  • 1950s: Busby Babes rise; tragedy and rebuild.
  • 1968: First European Cup win (Best, Law, Charlton).
  • 1999: Historic Treble — league, cup, Europe.
  • 2003–09: Ronaldo era, more titles and a UCL trophy.
  • Today: New chapters with academy talent and global support.

Why I chose this

I love football, and United is the team that made me fall in love with the sport. Ronaldo inspired me first, and then I learned the club’s deeper story — youth, courage, and success. This collage shows those pieces together.
